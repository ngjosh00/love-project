LISTEN. UNDERSTAND. VALIDATE.

Love is about giving. It is about giving yourself fully to another person and your relationship even when things aren't always looking up.

Love is about commitment. Committing to another person and their struggles and helping them achieve their happiness.

Love is about YOU. Love is about making sure you are loved first, that way you know how you can better love others.


What is LUV?

  1. Listen first. Stop talking. Quiet your mind and find peace and take in what is being said you.
  2. Understand all of them. The feelings, thoughts, passion, emotions, EVERYTHING. Dive deep.
  3. Validate them fully. Let them know they are heard, understood, and valued for being themselves.
Love Passionately. Love Deeply.

BE COMMITTED.
